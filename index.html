My life in Seoul - SOUTH KOREA

Seoul was the expatriation that stood out the most for me.

First, Seoul is known for its strong soft-power: K-pop, K-dramas. But also for its royal palaces like Gyeongbokgung palace, Buddhist temples, bright colors, curvy roods.

Second, the food is really good. I love kimchi, a spicy, fermented cabbage dish that will set your taste buds on fire (in a good way), and also bibimbap, my favorite dish, and delicious kimbap.

Moreover, there are convenient stores on every corner, they are open 24/7. So if you have a small craving of noodles at 2 a.m., no problem!

Finally, I loved working at coffee shops and doing Photoshoot in cabins with my friends and family. These spots are very popular in Korea.
